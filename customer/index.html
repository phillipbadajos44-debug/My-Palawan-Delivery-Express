🆘 Help & Support
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does delivery take? ›
Delivery time depends on the merchant's location and how busy our riders are, but most orders arrive within 30-60 minutes.
What payment methods are accepted? ›
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, and Maya. You can choose your preferred method at checkout.
Can I cancel my order? ›
You can cancel an order while it's still pending. Once the merchant has accepted it, please contact support for help.
How do I track my order? ›
Go to the Orders tab to see real-time status updates. Once a rider picks up your order, you can also chat with them directly.
What if my order arrives damaged or incorrect? ›
Please contact our support team right away with your order ID and a photo of the issue, and we'll help resolve it.
